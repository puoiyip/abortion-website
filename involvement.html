Educate and Inform Others

There are many misconceptions surrounding the topic of abortion - it's important for everyone to stay informed on the facts, regardless of their opinion on the topic. There are a variety of resources available to share to learn more and help others.

Write to State Politicians

These individuals are meant to represent their constituents, whom they are reliant on for re-election. They play a significant role in policymaking, both on the national and state level.

Ideas to include in a letter:


  • Advocate for the construction of more abortion clinics near state borders to aid women who are traveling out-of-state for an abortion

  • Inform your representative about the consequences of abortion bans and restrictions

  • Petition for abortion access to be recognized in your state's constitution
